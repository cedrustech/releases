Desai Trade - Your Business's Sales & Stock Companion

    Cedrus Tech, is proud to introduce 'Desai Trade', a custom Salesforce app tailored specifically for our esteemed client, Shree Enterprises, located in Mumbai City. Shree Enterprises, led by its dedicated owner, Arun Desai, is a Mumbai-based business that specializes in the distribution of various products. Before adopting 'Desai Trade', the company faced numerous challenges related to manual processes and lacked the tools to provide seamless service to their customers. This innovative application streamlines and enhances various aspects of Shree Enterprises' stock management and sales processes.


Challenges:

    Shree Enterprises and its team encountered several issues before implementing Desai Trade:

  1. Manual Invoicing: The team had to invest a considerable amount of time and effort in manually creating invoices using Excel spreadsheets. This process was not only time-consuming but also prone to errors.

  2. Inefficient Entry Management: Managing and tracking entries for stock materials, sales, and purchases was a complex task. This manual effort often led to discrepancies and inaccuracies.

  3. Monthly Reconciliation: At the end of each month, Shree Enterprises struggled to obtain accurate information on total sales, GST liabilities, and overall profitability. This made financial planning and compliance challenging.

  4. Customer Service: Providing seamless service to their customers was a challenge due to the lack of a streamlined system for managing stock and material details.


Key Benefits of Desai Trade:

    With the implementation of Desai Trade, Shree Enterprises has experienced a transformation in their business operations:

  1. Consignment Stock Management: Desai Trade enables Shree Enterprises to efficiently store and organize consignment stock materials, including crucial details such as quantity, price, and consignment names.

  2. Inventory Tracking: Users can record selling entries, inward and outward entries, which are automatically integrated with the inventory. This ensures real-time updates on total quantity, available quantity, and sold-out quantity.

  3. Invoice Generation: The app simplifies the invoicing process by automatically generating invoices based on sales transactions. It calculates taxes (GST) and allows users to apply discounts either in cash or as a percentage.

  4. Real-time Financial Insights: The app provides real-time data on total sales, GST calculations, and profitability, making monthly reconciliations a breeze.

  5. Report Generation: This app empowers Shree Enterprises to create customized reports tailored to their specific business needs. These reports can provide insights into sales trends, stock levels, and financial performance.

  6. Invoice Copies: The application allows the easy generation of invoice copies, ensuring that Shree Enterprises can quickly provide their customers with detailed and professional invoices.

  7. Enhanced Customer Service: Shree Enterprises can now provide their customers with prompt and accurate stock and material details by generating quick reports directly from the application.

    Desai Trade designed and developed by Cedrus Tech, has empowered Shree Enterprises and its dedicated team to overcome the challenges they once faced with manual processes. With this innovative Salesforce app, they have streamlined their invoicing, entry management, and financial tracking, enabling them to provide improved customer service. Shree Enterprises is now better equipped to meet the demands of their business and ensure smooth operations in the competitive business landscape of Mumbai.
